    Walker Kessler Early Life and Birth Details

    Walker Kessler was born on 26th July 2001 in Atlanta, Georgia, U.S. He is an American citizen. Not many details are known about his early life. Kessler had a family that was interested in basketball. Having a tall structure and an interest in basketball, Kessler found out that his call was in the field of basketball.

    Walker Kessler Education

    Kessler completed high school at Woodward Academy in College Park, Georgia. He began his career playing for them. After completing high school, Kessler was noted for his in-game skills and was offered a college education by many universities. Universities of North Carolina, Duke, Michigan, Virginia, and Vanderbilt offered him opportunities. He chose North Carolina Tar Heels over all of them and played for them for the 2020-21 season. Then he was transferred to Auburn Tigers. He played for the 2021-22 season and completed his college education with them.

    Walker Kessler Family and Parents

    Kessler hails from a family with a high basketball background. His father, Chad Kessler, and uncle, Alec, played college basketball during their early years. Kessler also has a sibling, Houston, who plays at local basketball tournaments. His mother’s name is Andrea Kessler.

    Walker Kessler Basketball Career and Playing Positions

    While in high school, Kessler played for the Woodward Academy. He averaged 17.8 points along with 9.3 rebounds and 5.2 blocks per game. He received many accolades for his game the Mr. Georgia Basketball Gatorade Player of the Year award. Kessler played for the North Carolina Tar Heels college team and set a freshman record by getting 16 points, 12 rebounds, and 8 blocks in a single game. He averaged 4.4 points and 3.2 rebounds for the team. He later played for the Auburn Tigers college team. Kessler starred as a defensive player on the team and received the SEC Defensive Player of the Year Award at the end of the season. He declared for the 2022 NBA draft on 3rd April 2022. Kessler currently plays for Utah Jazz. He plays at the Center position which helps him in being both an attacking player and a defensive player.

    Awards Won by Walker Kessler

    During his entire basketball career, Kessler has been awarded numerous awards and honors. While playing with the Woodward Academy, Kessler was the Player of the Year and was also selected in the McDonald’s All-American Selection. He also appeared in the Nike Hoop Summit of 2020.
