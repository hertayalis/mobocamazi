    After meeting at a 2015 charity event, the famous contractor failed to seal the deal by asking Kuznetsov for her digits. This led him on a wild goose chase to track down the blonde beauty. He was finally able to relocate her through social media and proceeded to stalk her Instagram profile for a few months before officially asking her out on a date. 

    "I felt this overwhelming urge to learn everything about Jacinta, but I didn't want to delve too deep on a first date and come across as pushy. So I asked about her career and fished for any similarities," Jonathan told People magazine.

    But there were no fireworks during that first outing, and they definitely weren't exchanging googly eyes either. Jonathan's tactic to bum rush Kuznetsov and dig for information caused her to blurt out, "Ugh! You ask the worst questions. Ask something interesting." Ouch.

    Jonathan cut the night short and apologized for their horrible outing. He suggested they have a first date redux the following night, and surprisingly, Kuznetsov took him up on his offer. "I promised to drop the drama and she pledged to set aside the sass. It turned out to be the beginning of something truly special," he said.

    Looking back on their rocky beginning, perhaps their failed first date was the universe's way of telling them to abort mission!
