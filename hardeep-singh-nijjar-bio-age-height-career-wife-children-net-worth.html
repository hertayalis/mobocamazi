    Hardeep Singh Nijjar Cause of Death

    Hardeep Singh Nijjar, a Khalistani terrorist who was wanted by the Indian government, was shot and killed in Surrey, Canada. Nijjar served as the chief of the Khalistan Tiger Force (KTF) and was one of 40 designated terrorists on the Indian government’s list.

    According to reports, Nijjar’s body was found with bullet wounds inside a car parked at the Guru Nanak Sikh Gurdwara Sahib in Surrey, where he served as the head.     Biography of Hardeep Singh Nijjar

    Hardeep Singh Nijjar was known for leading a campaign and spreading propaganda against India. He organized protests outside of Indian missions in several countries such as the US, UK, Germany, and Canada. He also allegedly ordered targeted killings in Punjab.

The SFJ was described as a front organization of Khalistan terrorist outfits operating from foreign soils including Pakistan.
