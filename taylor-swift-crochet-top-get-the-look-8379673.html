    I’ve been following Taylor Swift’s style for a long time — and I mean a long time. I’m talking about almost 15 years of research (err, should I say fangirling?) kind of time. Amidst her wardrobe are practical pieces like comfy cargo pants and little brown bags, along with high-end accessories and designer shoes. But sometimes, she throws a curve ball and gives us something unexpected.

    Last week, Taylor Swift was spotted having a casual girls’ night out with best friends Selena Gomez and Zoë Kravitz. The dress code was equally as chill, with Swift wearing a crochet top, black tank, high-waisted denim, and Anthropologie claw clips. The simple but elevated pieces worked together to create a totally relatable ‘fit. 

    While Swift’s crochet top may seem more like a spring or summer piece, it actually makes for a great fall layering staple, too. We don’t know exactly where Swift’s cute top is from, but I found six lookalikes to steal the pop star’s look. 

    Taylor Swift-Inspired Crochet Tops

    Goranbon Crochet Knit Sweater

    Currently on sale for $25 thanks to an on-site coupon is the Goranbon knit sweater. The crochet top features the same cropped length Swift’s original blouse boasts, while mimicking the scalloped hemline and cutout design. The neutral color is additionally great for pairing with a wide array of closet staples, such as jeans, suede pants, knee-high boots, and even wide-brim hats. Shop the discounted blouse in four various hues and sizes S to XXL. 

    Runcati Fishnet Crochet Crop Top

    Runcati’s fishnet crop top is another stellar choice. The bell sleeves give a nod to Swift’s OG style, while the wide boat neck only makes it an even better lookalike. You can make this top even more seasonally appropriate by adding a fitted long-sleeve shirt underneath or layering on a leather jacket. It won’t take away from the overall boho aesthetic, but only enhance the look. Choose from sizes S to XL and colors like white, khaki, and black.    

    Aqua Crochet Scallop Crop Top

    If you’re all about the Midnights singer’s style but want something a bit more conservative, then consider Aqua’s scallop top. It includes the same staple elements, such as the long sleeves, curved bottom, crochet design, and pullover style, but shows less skin. This makes the top easier to wear to work, dinners, and everything in-between. Dress it up with a maple-colored skirt, nude heels, and a sleek mini bag.
