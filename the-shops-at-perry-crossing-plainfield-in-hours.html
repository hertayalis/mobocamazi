    What Time Does The Shops at Perry Crossing Open and Close?

    The Shops at Perry Crossing opens their doors at 10:00 AM every day of the week, Monday through Sunday. The closing times vary by day, with the exception of Sunday, when the shopping center closes earlier than other days.

    Is The Shops at Perry Crossing Open on Weekends?

    Yes, The Shops at Perry Crossing is open on weekends. In fact, it’s a popular destination for weekend shopping trips. On Saturdays, the shopping center is open from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM. However, on Sundays, the hours of operation are shorter, and the center is open from 12:00 PM to 6:00 PM.

    The Shops at Perry Crossing Hours of Operation

    Thursday: 10:00 AM – 9:00 PM
