    The renowned American professional boxer Shakur Stevenson has left a lasting impression on the boxing community. He has won two world titles in weight divisions in the past. He wore the prestigious WBO featherweight belt from 2019 until 2020.

    Shakur continued his incredible journey by winning the WBO super featherweight title in 2021–2022, solidifying his status as a rising star in the fight industry.

    The boxer’s career started full of promise when he competed for the United States at the 2016 Summer Olympics, winning a silver medal in the bantamweight division.

    February 2017 marked his professional debut. Stevenson’s skill was obvious even before he made his professional boxing debut, as he won the gold in the flyweight class at the 2014 AIBA Youth World Boxing Championships.
