    The influencer sorority girlfriend of Ravens tight end Mark Andrews posted a heartfelt message Wednesday to her NFL beau after the team’s AFC Championship loss to the Chiefs over the weekend.

    Taking to Instagram, Elena Yates shared a handful of photos of the couple throughout the season, which featured the TikTok personality sporting Ravens gear.

    “Loved watching you do what you do best,” Yates, 21, wrote with a purple heart emoji. “Can’t wait to be back & better than ever!! Until next season.”

    Andrews, 28, shared a heart eyes emoji in the comments.

    The couple started dating last summer and spent their first Christmas together in December, Yates said in a TikTok video ahead of her sorority’s winter formal.

    Yates is a member of the Delta Delta Delta sorority at the College of Charleston, according to her social media.

    “I’ve been keeping my life a little more private,” Yates said in a “get ready with me” video on TikTok in October. “… I’ve been going to a lot of football games.

    “My boyfriend is a tight end on the Baltimore Ravens, which is fun… I don’t know what to say because it’s my first time talking about this.

    “We’ve been dating for four months, like a week after I turned 21 we went out to dinner, it was great, [we] hit it off… My roommates and I, we watch his games every Sunday.”

    Yates also gushed over the other Ravens’ wives and girlfriends.

    “I honestly love all the other girlfriends and wives on the team. It’s a really good community and everyone is so nice and inclusive.”

    At the time, Yates said she took a step back from social media for privacy reasons.

    “It’s just scary when you date someone or you’re involved just with anyone that’s in the spotlight like that,” she said. I just want to respect people’s privacy. I think that’s a big thing.”

    The Moorestown, N.J., native is in her senior year at the College of Charleston, where she is majoring in marketing with a focus on fashion and branding, according to her LinkedIn.

    Andrews does not have any photos with Yates on social media.
